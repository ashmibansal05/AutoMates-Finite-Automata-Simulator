Our Mission

AutoMates is dedicated to making the concepts of finite automata and computational theory accessible and engaging for students, educators, and enthusiasts.

Who We Are

We are a team of passionate students and developers from the Theory of Computation course, united by our fascination with the elegance of automata theory. Our project, AutoMates, is a culmination of our efforts to create an interactive platform that simplifies the understanding of finite automata through visualization and hands-on tools.

What We Do

AutoMates provides an intuitive simulator for designing, testing, and exploring finite automata. Whether you're learning about deterministic finite automata (DFA), non-deterministic finite automata (NFA), or regular expressions, our platform offers tools to bring these concepts to life. We aim to bridge the gap between theoretical computer science and practical application.

Our Vision

We envision a world where complex computational concepts are demystified through interactive learning. By developing AutoMates, we hope to inspire the next generation of computer scientists to explore the beauty and power of theoretical computation.
